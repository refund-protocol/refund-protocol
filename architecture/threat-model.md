# Threat Model

REFUND assumes the presence of adversarial actors.

Considered threats include:
- Malicious token deployers
- Coordinated liquidity removal
- Sybil participation in claims
- Protocol gaming attempts

Mitigations include:
- Deterministic eligibility constraints
- Batched execution
- Reduced discretionary surfaces
- Limited exposure of internal logic

Certain mitigations are intentionally undocumented.
